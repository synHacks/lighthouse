What is synHacks Lighthouse?

synHacks Lighthouse is a Classroom Mentorship program created by to connect inspiring students in computer science classrooms with CS mentors in college. synHacks Lighthouse works very closely with CS teachers and mentors to provide students with resources and direct support. synHacks Lighthouse works directly with a teacher’s curriculum making a seamless interaction.  

synHacks Lighthouse provides students with

Expert aid and support from college mentors

Connections to count on inside and outside the virtual classroom

A push to understand the concepts rather than just know how to use them
